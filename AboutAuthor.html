About the Author:

Jacob K. Sallow

Jacob Sallow was a young student at Canondale University when he and his classmate, Benedict Furtenchaft, first began writing novellas together back in 2004, publishing indie projects on the now inactive "Lettering Images" website. They founded The Kischur-Sallow team in the summer of 2012, after forming a small friend group with fellow writers Christopher Penkin, Villa Cuomo, and Jules Herbert.

Together, they wrote and published titles such as "They've lost the plot" and "Right back at you", which won Ink & Mirrors awards in 2015 and 2016, respectively. The team continues to write to this day, while Sallow writes in this webpage during his freetime, usually about his current interests.
